Buttons

Links to social media and others

These designed buttons follow the color code. They have links to all the corresponding websites except for the name logo. They are placed in a blue bar on the left that is fixed and a pink bar at the bottom that goes down as the user scrolls. They are also placed in the biography itself. The name logo is placed above the model's name in Chaturbate. They scale with the user's screen.

Referral

GIF for free tokens

The Chaturbate's referral campaign allows the model to win 10 tokens for each new user registered through the link attached to the GIF. The choice of a GIF over a simple image is useful to attract the attention of a new user.

Lists

Fan Club and Big Tips

The bio contains 2 lists, one for the fan club and another one for record tips. They are highlighted by a background GIF. They can be easily edited by the model, and come with a scrolling option if they are more entries.

The bio has a section to sell highlight better videos sold in the in-built chaturbate shop. The GIFs that illustrate the videos were sent by the model as a short teasing video. They are modifiyable by the model following the "manual" delivered with the bio. The list can scale to the number of videos the model wants to highlight.
